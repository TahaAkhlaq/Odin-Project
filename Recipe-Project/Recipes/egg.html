Sunny Side Up Eggs

Description

To make a delicious sunny side up egg, start by cracking a fresh egg into a bowl. Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Carefully slide the cracked egg into the pan, making sure not to break the yolk. Allow the egg to fry gently until the whites are set and the edges turn golden brown, usually about 2-3 minutes. Using a spatula, carefully transfer the cooked egg onto a plate. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and enjoy your sunny side up egg while it's still warm and the yolk is beautifully runny.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Crack a fresh egg into a bowl.
  2. Preheat a non-stick pan and add a drizzle of olive oil.
  3. Carefully slide the cracked egg into the pan.
  4. Fry the egg until the whites are set and the edges turn golden brown, approximately 2-3 minutes.
  5. Transfer the cooked egg onto a plate, season with salt and pepper to taste, and enjoy your sunny side up egg.
