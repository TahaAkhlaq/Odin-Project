Cream Cheese Sandwich

Description

To make a simple cream cheese sandwich, start by toasting two slices of bread until golden brown. Then, spread a generous layer of plain cream cheese on one side of each slice. Place the slices together, cream cheese sides facing each other, and gently press to create a sandwich. Enjoy your delicious and straightforward cream cheese sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread until they are golden brown.
  2. On one side of each slice, spread a generous layer of plain cream cheese.
  3. Place the slices together with the cream cheese sides facing each other.
  4. Gently press the slices together to create a sandwich.
  5. Enjoy your delicious cream cheese sandwich!
